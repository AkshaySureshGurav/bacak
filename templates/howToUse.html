How to use

This is a basic accounting WebApp where you'll find three accounts namely -

1. Cash account - is base to all accounting work in this WebApp. Because if you want to show outstanding balance/ expense then you need cash balance in your hand.

2. Outstanding account - is an account where you'll find list of accounts which have outstanding balances.

3. Expense account - is an account where you'll find all the expenses made.

First step will be to add the cash balance so that we can show outstanding balances and expenses. After adding cash balance, you'll need to make accounts of the individual/company to whom you are giving cash as outstanding balance or as an expense. You can make accounts of an individual/company from Make accounts option on the Homepage.

After making the accounts, you can make entries to outstanding (O/s) account or expense account. Even if you are making an entry to expense account, you will need to make an entry to outstanding account first. Because, a entry will not be considered as an expense untill you have a receipt or acknowledge paper from the opposite party for the expense.

You can view the accounts by selecting the View accounts option on the Homepage. If you want to check a sample accounts in this WebApp, I have made a sample account with Username="xyz.LTD" and password="xyz" in which I have made entries to the accounts for giving an example how the entries are done.
