About the developer and WebApp

Hi! Akshay this side, the developer of this WebApp. Before studying Computer science, I had a degree in Accounting and finance and worked as a branch accountant for 1 and a half year. As a branch accountant, my work there was to manually update the outstanding, cash and expense book which was 9 hours of work daily.


This WebApp was made as an attempt to automate that accounting work and reduce the work time to less than an half hour. The app is specifically made for doing the accounting work as per my experience as a branch accountant and as of now it is not very dynamic in functioning.
